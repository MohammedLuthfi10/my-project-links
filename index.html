Work Experience

I have 1.3 years of experience in embedded development, including bare-metal programming for microcontrollers like dsPIC33CK256mp508. During this time, I have developed low-level drivers for ADC, PWM, UART, I2C, SPI, TIMER, and GPIO. My work also includes creating a driver for the DRV8305 motor driver and using it to control a BLDC motor. Additionally, I have experience in writing Linux kernel modules, custom OS development for Raspberry Pi, and implementing communication protocols like CAN FD and I2C.
